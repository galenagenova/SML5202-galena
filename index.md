<h2>Ode to Autumn</h2>
<p>
 SEASON of mists and mellow fruitfulness,<br>
 Close bosom-friend of the maturing sun;<br>
 Conspiring with him how to load and bless<br>
 With fruit the vines that round the thatch-eaves run;
</p>
